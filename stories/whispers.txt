Sir Past played a game with Ma Present
Written letters and bottled notes
Cast to sea, buried in earth

A game called chinese whispers

Participants are the people of Time
Ago, Before, Eon, Epoch, Yesterday, Today
Every player a reflection of another
They look like you, move like me, sound like us
Yet we find them difficult to accept

Messages recieved but contents misconstrued
Twisted as they are by passage through Times
Hard won lessons, carved in stone
Crack with age, hole ridden and worn

Ma Present will play with The Future
The same game of whispers
She hopes the holes have been filled in proper

We, the people of time, will try to understand
"Why is our foundation leaking water?"
